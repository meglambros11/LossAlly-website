— Our Purpose

Death leaves a mountain of to‑dos. You shouldn't climb it alone.

When someone we love dies, the world doesn't pause. Bills arrive. Institutions demand paperwork. Strangers need to be called. Accounts need closing. Most families are completely unprepared for the administrative avalanche — and it falls at the worst possible moment.

Loss Ally was founded by a daughter who watched her mother lose a sister and figure out the aftermath entirely alone. She created a service that sits beside families — not just at the legal level, but at the human one — guiding every step with clarity and warmth.

Day 1–3 — First Call

Your dedicated advisor reaches out to listen, assess, and prioritize the immediate needs.

Week 1 — Urgent Tasks

Death certificates, employer notification, stopping recurring charges, securing the home.

Month 1 — Financial

Banks, insurance, pension, Social Security, investment accounts — all coordinated.

Months 2–6 — Estate

Probate navigation, property transfer, tax filings, digital asset closure.

Closure — Peace of Mind

Final estate summary, record-keeping package, and a clear declaration of completion.
